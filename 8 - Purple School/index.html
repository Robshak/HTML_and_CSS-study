A critic at work is your friend, but not always
When you do a code review, sometimes you are met with the fact that you give constructive criticism and it is received with hostility, as if it were a personal insult. The person tries with all his might to resist feedback and is not ready to listen to arguments. There are two options: either the feedback was given incorrectly, or the person does not accept criticism. In this post I want to focus on how to deal with criticism.
